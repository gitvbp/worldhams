Data Deletion Request

Developer: WorldHams

App Name: WorldHams App (com.worldhams.main)

How to Request Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and personal data associated with the WorldHams App, please send us an email:

Email: worldhamsapp@gmail.com

Please include the email address used in the app and a brief note requesting deletion.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your account and profile information
  • App-specific data linked to your account
  • Any stored content you created or submitted in the app

What Data May Be Retained?

Some anonymous or aggregated data may be retained for analytics or legal reasons, but it will no longer be linked to your account.

Deletion Timeframe

Data will be deleted within 7 business days after confirmation of your request.
